Motivation

 

Let's talk about what motivates us

Extrinsic

  • Comes from outside you
  • Could motivate you to do any kind of work
  • Valid, often essential
  • Strangely, some things, like money, can both motivate and demotivate

Intrinsic

  • Comes from inside you
  • Often comes from the work itself
  • Very, very powerful
  • Most powerful:
    • Autonomy
    • Mastery
    • Sense of purpose

You are entering a profession where:
